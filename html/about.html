We provide custom cloud services such as SAAS, StAAS, IAAS.

SAAS

Software as a service, is a software distribution model in which application are hosted by service provider and is made availabe to customer over a network. The cloud provider host and manages the software application and underlying infrastructure. We will be providing a browser(firefox), a camera, a text editor.

StAAS

Storage as a service,is generally seen as an alternative for small or mid-size business that lacks the capital budget or technical personnel to implement and maintain their own storage infrastructure.

IAAS

Infrastructure as a service,involves outsourcing the equipment used to support operation, including storage, hardware, server and networking components, which is made accessible over a network. we will be providing various operating system(ubuntu,redhat,wibdows, etc).A virtual machine of desired operating system will be installed in clients machine.
